Speak To Me, Speak For Me

Sometimes I wonder, how can this stranger make something so intimate to me? She sings of things I have yet to experience, but somehow they resonate with me. She reveals parts of me that I did not know existed. She inspires new parts of me; she inspires my desire to also communicate and express such intimate feelings and messages.

Her art speaks to me in a way that is distant yet personal, and I appreciate her message. I, too, want to speak to those distant from me. Do my feelings and thoughts resonate with others? Can we connect through an indescribable yet mutual understanding? Can my art speak for me in this way?

Honestly, my art is usually not that deep. I usually either draw still lives for class assignments or just sketch female faces, but I really do want to make greater things. I hope that through Mwellon, I can develop my art into something greater.
